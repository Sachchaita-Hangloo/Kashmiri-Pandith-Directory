Welcome to the Global Kashmiri Pandit Directory

This directory aims to connect Kashmiri Pandits across the globe. Whether you're looking to reconnect with community members from your original village, find Pandits in your current location, or participate in cultural events, this platform can help you rebuild and strengthen our community bonds.

How it works:

  1. Add your information - Share your current location and your original Kashmir residence
  2. Find nearby Pandits - Connect with community members near your current location
  3. Digital Village - Reconnect with people from your original village or city in Kashmir
  4. Community Events - Discover and participate in cultural gatherings, religious ceremonies, and social events
  5. Host an Event - Create and promote your own community gathering

This platform was created to strengthen our cultural bonds, preserve our heritage, and rebuild our community across geographical distances. The Kashmiri Pandit diaspora may be spread worldwide, but through this directory, we can maintain our connections and shared identity.
